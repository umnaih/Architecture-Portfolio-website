    Welcome to Minima Studio

    an architecture firm that has been designing and building since 1980.
    As a part of our firm's history, we have completed numerous projects in various locations, providing unique and innovative design solutions to our clients.

    Our team at Minima Studio

    Our team comprises skilled architects, designers, and project managers who are passionate about creating functional and aesthetic spaces.
    Our philosophy revolves around the idea that simplicity and elegance can coexist, and we strive to create spaces that are both timeless and modern.

    Our Company is Inspired by Zaha Hadid

    Architecture is like writing. You have to edit it over and over so it looks effortless.

    Zaha Hadid's innovative designs and boundary-pushing structures left a significant impact on the world of architecture, establishing her as a prominent figure in the field as an Iraqi architect.

    At Minima Studio

    We specialize in minimalist design, which is characterized by its clean lines, simple shapes, and use of natural materials.
    We believe that minimalism is more than just a design aesthetic; it is a way of life that promotes mindfulness, sustainability, and efficiency.
